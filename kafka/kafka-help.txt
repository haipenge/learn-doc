1.安装
  Set JAVA_HOME
  Set SCALA_HOME
2.下载
   kafka.apache.org
3. 常用命令
  1).启动ZK
    bin/zookeeper-server-start.sh -daemon config/zookeeper.properties
  2).启动kafka
     bin/kafka-server-start.sh -daemon config/server.properties
  3).Create Topic
     bin/kafka-topics.sh --create --zookeeper localhost:2181 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1 --topic default-msg
  4).List Topic
     bin/kafka-topics.sh --list --zookeeper localhost:2181
  5).Send Message
     bin/kafka-console-producer.sh --broker-list localhost:9092 --topic default-msg
  6).Receive Msg
     bin/kafka-console-consumer.sh --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic default-msg --from-beginning
